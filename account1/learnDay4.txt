Day 4 of AI in Healthcare: The Future of Medicine Journey! Today, I delved into the incredible power of Predictive Analytics in healthcare. Beyond just diagnosing existing conditions, I learned how AI can forecast future health events, transforming reactive medicine into proactive care.
